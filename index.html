Thai Classic Massage

Experience the authentic Thai Classic Massage, a traditional healing practice that has been refined over centuries. This ancient technique combines acupressure, stretching, and energy work to restore balance and harmony to your body and mind.

Traditional Techniques:

  • Sen line energy work along the body's meridians
  • Passive yoga-like stretching movements
  • Pressure point massage using palms, thumbs, and elbows
  • Joint mobilization and flexibility enhancement

What Makes It Special:

No Oils Used

Traditional Thai massage is performed fully clothed without oils, allowing for dynamic stretching movements.

Energy Balancing

Focus on balancing the body's energy systems through targeted pressure and movement.

Holistic Approach

Treats the whole person, addressing physical, mental, and spiritual well-being.

Session Information:

Duration: 90-120 minutes recommended for full benefits

Preparation: Wear comfortable, loose-fitting clothing

Best For: Those seeking deep relaxation and increased flexibility
